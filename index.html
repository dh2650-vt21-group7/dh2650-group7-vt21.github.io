Hungry Rush

A cute 2D-plateformer-runner with a great hook mechanic

Play it now on itch.io

Meet the character

This is Harry. Hungry Harry to be precise. He's on a quest to collect his favorite cereals. To help him, he has his favorite fork that attaches to everything.

Watch out for the dangers

It's not without obstacles that Harry must get out of the cave and retrieve all his cereal packages

Use Harry's fantastic arm

To escape the awful cave and collect all cereal packages Harry can use his fantastic arm. It's flexible yet strong and together with his fork he can swing through the cave escaping mean coconuts and the water that is constantly rising.

Reaching the surface

When you help Harry escaping the surfaces he can finally eat is cereal knowing that he has escaped the unpleasant cave
